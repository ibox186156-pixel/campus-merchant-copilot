# Campus Merchant Copilot

Campus Merchant Copilot is a micro-AI opportunity validation project for university-area merchants such as milk tea shops, light-meal stores, print shops, stationery shops and parcel kiosks.

The product concept is a finance-aware AI operator that turns messy merchant data into:

- daily operating actions,
- cash-flow warnings,
- inventory and promotion recommendations,
- and credit-ready evidence without claiming loan approval.

## Opportunity Summary

Target users are 1-5 person micro merchants around campuses. Their core pains are thin cash buffers, intuitive inventory decisions, weak promotion discipline and fragmented financing evidence.

The timing is attractive because payments, delivery orders and reviews are already digitized, while OCR and financial reasoning models can convert messy shop traces into daily actions and finance-readiness explanations.

## MVP Workflow

1. Ingest payment exports, receipt photos, delivery orders, inventory notes and reviews.
2. Normalize with OCR, category mapping, supplier matching and margin estimation.
3. Reason over cash gap, waste risk, promotion fit and finance-readiness.
4. Act with three daily recommendations, group-chat copy, purchase list and evidence memo.

## Two-Week Validation Gate

- 15 merchant interviews.
- 30 anonymized receipts or daily summaries.
- 5 pilot shops receiving daily briefs.
- Daily brief open rate over 60%.
- Action execution rate over 30%.
- 2 prepaid pilots and 1 channel/data partner commitment.
